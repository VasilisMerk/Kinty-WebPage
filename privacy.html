Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Kinty is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal data when you use our mobile app (the "Service").

2. Definitions

"Personal Data" means any identifiable information about you. "Service" means the Kinty mobile app. "Third-Party Services" means external vendors we use to operate the Service, such as Google Analytics and Firebase.

3. Collection of Personal Data

Kinty collects name, email, IP address, cookies, and analytics data, including usage patterns and device information, through Google Analytics.

4. Use of Personal Data

We use Personal Data to provide and improve the Service, communicate with users, analyze usage patterns, and comply with applicable laws.

5. Sharing of Personal Data

We may share Personal Data with Third-Party Services, such as Google Analytics, RevenueCat, and Firebase, and with law enforcement where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We will retain Personal Data for as long as necessary to provide our Service, or as required by law.

7. Security Measures

We implement reasonable security protections to safeguard your data, though no security measures can guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookie Policy

Cookies are used to collect analytics data. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, object to processing of, and port your Personal Data.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
