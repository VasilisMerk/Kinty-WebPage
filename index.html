Features

Everything you need to keep your relationships alive

✦

Spontaneous, not scheduled

Instead of a fixed date, Kinty nudges you within a natural time window — like "once every 2–3 weeks" — so reaching out never feels like a chore.

  • Randomized nudges within your chosen frequency
  • See what's overdue, due today, or coming up
  • Complete or renew a reminder in one tap
👥

Import your contacts in seconds

Pick a frequency, then choose contacts straight from your address book. Bulk-import as many people as you want, each with the cadence that fits them.

  • Five built-in frequency presets, from very frequent to rare
  • Or set a fully custom interval
  • Bulk import instead of adding contacts one by one
🎂

Stay close to every celebration

Birthdays are tracked automatically once a contact's date is set, with a dedicated view for who's celebrating this week and who's coming up next.

  • This-week and upcoming birthday views
  • Countdown to each birthday
  • Notifications so you never miss the day
💬

Pick up right where you left off

Jot down what you last talked about — a new job, an upcoming trip — so your next conversation starts warm instead of with "so, what's new?"

  • Searchable notes per contact
  • Recent topics surfaced automatically
  • Quick-add right after a reminder is completed
☺

Manage every connection

A single place to see all your contacts and their reminder frequency, editable anytime as relationships change.

  • Edit frequency per contact whenever you like
  • Search across all contacts
  • Multiple app themes to match your style
How it works

Three steps to never losing touch

STEP 01

Add your people

Import contacts from your address book or add them one at a time.

STEP 02

Pick a frequency

Choose how often you want to reach out — from very frequently to rarely, or set a custom window.

STEP 03

Get nudged, naturally

Kinty reminds you within that window, at a time you choose, so it never feels forced.
